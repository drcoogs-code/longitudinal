Precision Preventive Medicine

Family Medicine Consultants. Longevity specialists.

As one of the UK’s most established providers of longevity-focused primary care, we have brought together clinical depth, advanced diagnostics and disciplined interpretation to create our Signature Health Assessment.

This is not a one-day medical.

Health does not reveal itself in haste.
Risk cannot be understood without context.
Investigation should follow consultation — not replace it.

At our Centre of Clinical Excellence in Antrobus, your assessment is structured, unhurried and GP-led. Time is given to history. Physiology is measured properly. Imaging is used with purpose. Results are interpreted in depth.

The outcome is clarity — not simply data.

Designed to protect healthspan over decades, not days.

myGP clinic Signature Health Assessment Philosophy
01 Depth Before Speed Most assessments are a snapshot; ours is a deep-dive interrogation. We prioritize the "why" over the "what," ensuring that every data point is contextualized within your unique biological narrative before we act.
02 Consultant-Led Medicine You are not managed by a protocol or a technician. Every decision is filtered through the clinical intuition of a senior specialist, ensuring that complex data is translated into nuanced, human-centric medical strategy.
03 Cardiovascular Intelligence We go beyond standard blood pressure and cholesterol. Using advanced hemodynamics and structural imaging, we map the real-time health of your vascular system to preempt events decades before they occur.
04 Cancer Vigilance Early detection is the only true cure. We utilize multi-modal screening—from liquid biopsies to whole-body imaging—to identify cellular shifts at the earliest possible stage of intercept.
05 Brain Longevity Cognitive health is the ultimate currency of aging. We assess neuro-vascular integrity and metabolic brain health to preserve executive function, memory, and mental agility for the long term.
06 Metabolic Precision Metabolism is the engine of aging. We analyze glucose dynamics, hormonal balance, and mitochondrial efficiency to optimize how your body processes energy and manages systemic inflammation.
07 Advanced Imaging We see what others miss. By utilizing the highest resolution MRI and CT protocols available, we create a transparent view of your internal landscape, removing the guesswork from preventive care.
08 Physiological Reserve It isn't just about being "not sick"; it's about being robust. We measure your body’s capacity to withstand stress—testing cardiorespiratory fitness and musculoskeletal integrity to ensure you remain high-functioning.
09 Lifestyle Architecture Health isn't found in a pill; it is built in the gaps between appointments. We provide a structural blueprint for your sleep, nutrition, and movement, engineered to fit the demands of a high-performance life.
10 Psychological Resilience A high-functioning body requires a regulated nervous system. We integrate neuro-performance and stress-management metrics to ensure your mental state supports your physical longevity goals.
11 Data-Guided Insight Data without direction is noise. We aggregate your biomarkers into a clean, actionable dashboard, allowing us to make evidence-based adjustments to your plan with surgical precision.
12 Longitudinal Partnership We are the stewards of your health over time. This is not a one-off check-up, but a continuous, evolving collaboration designed to adapt as your biology and your life goals change.

myGP clinic Signature Health

ASSESSMENT
Clinical Foundation
Pre-Assessment & Risk Profiling
Comprehensive evidence-based questionnaires completed in advance, covering sleep, stress, mood, physical activity, nutrition and cardiovascular risk. Establishes behavioural, metabolic and hereditary risk before you step into the room.
Consultant-Led Longevity Consultation
A dedicated one-to-one consultation with a Family Medicine Consultant specialising in preventive and longevity medicine. Detailed medical history, family risk mapping, medication review and healthspan strategy discussion — depth before diagnostics.
Full Systems Clinical Examination
A structured, consultant-standard examination across cardiovascular, respiratory, abdominal, neurological and musculoskeletal systems. Clinical findings guide investigation — not the other way round.
Personalised Investigation Strategy
Risk-stratified discussion of appropriate imaging, physiological testing and laboratory analysis. Every investigation is purposeful, clinically indicated and aligned to long-term health optimisation.
Physiological & Imaging Assessment
Baseline Physiological Function Assessment
A structured measurement of core physiological markers including blood pressure, anthropometrics, body composition, resting ECG, lung function and comprehensive laboratory analysis. Establishes objective metabolic and cardiovascular baseline data.
Targeted Ultrasound Imaging
Detailed ultrasound examination of key organs and vascular structures including thyroid, carotids, abdominal organs and aorta. Radiation-free imaging to detect structural and early vascular change.
Advanced Whole-Body MRI
Comprehensive MRI assessment of brain, thorax, abdomen and pelvis using wide-bore technology for comfort. High-resolution, radiation-free imaging for early detection of structural disease.
Advanced Cardiac Functional Assessment
Specialist cardiology testing including echocardiography and exercise treadmill testing (ETT). Assesses cardiac structure, valve function, exercise tolerance and inducible ischaemia.
Longevity Integration & Longitudinal Strategy
Consultant Integration & Clinical Interpretation
Your Longevity Consultant reviews and synthesises all findings — consultation data, examination findings, physiological metrics, imaging and specialist reports. Patterns are identified, risk is stratified, and incidental findings are interpreted in proper clinical context.
Results Consultation
A dedicated follow-up appointment with your Longevity Consultant to discuss findings in detail. Clear explanation. Clear priorities. Clear next steps. No raw data without interpretation.
Personalised Healthspan Strategy
A structured, individualised plan addressing cardiovascular, metabolic, cancer and cognitive risk — aligned to your physiology, lifestyle and family history. Interventions may include medical optimisation, lifestyle modification, further specialist referral or targeted monitoring.
Six-Month Longitudinal Review
A scheduled review with your Longevity Consultant to assess progress, adherence and biomarker response. Ensures accountability, course correction and sustained health improvement — not a one-off report.
